# Milestones (Passive Telemetry)

These milestones are achieved automatically when the rocket reaches certain conditions, without the need for manual data transmission. They represent significant "firsts" or achievements in the space program.

## Velocity Milestones
*   **Sound Barrier**: Reach defined speed of sound (e.g., 343 m/s).
*   **Hypersonic**: Reach 5x speed of sound.
*   **Orbital Velocity**: Reach sufficient horizontal velocity for stable orbit (~ 800-1000 m/s).
*   **Escape Velocity**: Reach velocity required to escape the planet's gravity.

## Altitude Milestones
*   **Upper Atmosphere**: Cross the boundary into the upper atmosphere (e.g., 1,320m).
*   **Karman Line (Space)**: Reach the official edge of space (e.g., 2,000m).
*   **High Orbit**: Reach a significant altitude above the planet (e.g., 10,000m).
*   **Moon Altitude**: Reach the orbital altitude of the moon.

## Latitude/Positional Milestones
*   **Equatorial Flight**: Maintain flight close to the equator for a duration.
*   **Polar Flyover**: Reach high latitudes (> 80 degrees).
*   **Global Circumnavigation**: Complete a full orbit around the planet.
